orkidAsIyey

ABOUT

orkidAsIyey is a website tool developed to help identifying Orchidaceae species across the Philippines. Offering an array of tools such as image, identification, chatbot assistance and a checklist/database of Orchidaceae in the Philippines.




PURPOSE

orkidAsIyey is made to bring accessibility and convenience to information and data regarding Orchidaceae in the Philippines.
