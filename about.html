ABOUT

orkidAsIyey is a website tool developed to help identify Orchidaceae species across the Philippines. Offering an array of tools such as image identification, chatbot assistance and pages of Orchidaceae in the Philippines with their taxonomic classification and more.




PURPOSE

orkidAsIyey was made to bring accessibility and convenience to information and data regarding Orchidaceae in the Philippines.
